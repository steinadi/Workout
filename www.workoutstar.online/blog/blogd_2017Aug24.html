7 TIPS HOW TO STAY MOTIVATED

Posted by Luisa Onnebrink

1. Set a goal – Ask yourself why you are doing this. Do you want to lose weight? Do you want to change your shape? Do you want to become stronger? Set yourself realistic short term goals to make progress more achieveable!

2. Monitor your progress – Take pictures and make frequent check- ups! This way you will be able to see when you reached your goals!

3. Fun it up – Do workouts that you like! Spice them up with challenges, bring in some variety. Maybe try a yoga workout on Monday, a crazy HIIT session on Tuesday and go for a run on Thursday? Keep it fun and interesting!

4. Dress for the Occasion – Wear something comfy! Nothing is worse than an itchy sports bra or see-through leggins during squats. You might even want to treat yourself to a piece of clothing that makes you feel great while kicking ass!

5. Buddy up – Having a friend to work out with can help you stick to your fitness plan. Making a commitment to someone will help you stay on track and not give up when it’s getting tough! And working out is just so much more fun when you have someone by your side!

6. Reward yourself – Don’t forget to give yourself credit for your accomplishments and hard work! Appreciate everything you’re doing! Maybe reward yourself with a nice smoothie after a workout or go to the awesome restaurant you always wanted to try. Or if you workout in the mornings, reward yourself with an amazing breakfast afterwards! You will feel amazing and energized!

7. Schedule a frequent workout time – Having a set date and time in your calendar makes your workout to a real appointment in your weekly schedule.
